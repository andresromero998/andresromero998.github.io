Economist & Political Economy Researcher

Andrés Romero is an economist specializing in political economy, quantitative analysis, and the evaluation of public and social policies, with a particular focus on informal markets, organized crime, underground economies, and corruption in South America. He has worked in economic consulting and policy analysis for international organizations and multinational companies.

He is currently completing his MSc in Political Science and Political Economy at the London School of Economics and Political Science, where he has specialized in econometric methods applied to political science, game theory, causal inference, observational studies, and the impact evaluation of policies and programs.
